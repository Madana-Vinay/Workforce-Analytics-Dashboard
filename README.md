# Workforce-Analytics-Dashboard
Designed an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard in Microsoft Excel that provides insights into employee attrition, workforce composition, job satisfaction, and departmental performance. Utilized advanced Excel features including Pivot Tables, KPI reporting, and data visualization to support strategic HR decision-making.

#  HR Analytics Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive workforce analytics solution built in Microsoft Excel to transform raw employee data into actionable business insights. The dashboard helps HR professionals and business leaders monitor workforce trends, employee attrition, job satisfaction, demographics, and departmental performance through dynamic visualizations and KPI-driven reporting.


## Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to analyze workforce data and identify key factors influencing employee attrition and organizational performance. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by providing a centralized view of critical HR metrics.


## Key Features

* Interactive dashboard with dynamic filtering
* Employee attrition analysis
* Workforce demographic insights
* Department-wise performance analysis
* Job role attrition tracking
* Job satisfaction monitoring
* KPI-based executive reporting
* User-friendly data visualization

## Business Insights

### Employee Attrition Analysis

* Overall attrition rate is 16.12%.
* Identifies workforce segments with higher turnover.
* Supports employee retention initiatives.

### Age Group Analysis

* Employees aged 25–34 contribute the highest attrition.
* Highlights the need for career development and engagement programs.

### Department Analysis

* Research & Development and Sales departments experience higher employee turnover.
* Helps HR teams focus retention efforts on specific business units.

### Job Role Analysis

High attrition observed in:

* Laboratory Technician
* Sales Executive
* Research Scientist

### Workforce Demographics

The dashboard provides insights based on:

* Gender
* Marital Status
* Education Background
* Department
